How I found
my way in

Origins

Kerch, Ukraine. 1994.

Born between two seas, one landline, and zero patience for being bored.

In 2001, a 56K dialup screamed its way into our house and into my life. Mom hated it — I'd occupy our phone for hours. I thought it felt magical.

An entire universe on the other end of a cable, and me, impatient to break into it.

♫ Remember the sound?
Going places

Rapid City, SD. 2009.

As a teen: soldering robots, DC++ swaps, IRC friends I'd never meet, trading pirated game discs. It was clunky, slow, but now that I look back — kind of amazing.

In 2009, the family moved to Rapid City, SD. First real job at 16, American high school vibes, skiing on weekends, collecting rocks.

Back to Kerch in 2014 — older, sharper, same instinct to poke at things until they made sense.

The turning point

Porto, PT. 2020.

After years in product and sales, I realized the best work happens when you stop selling and start solving. That shift changed everything.

I moved to Porto, went remote-first, and started building the kind of client relationships I always wished I had — ones built on trust, product depth, and zero BS.

Now I help SaaS teams close deals, onboard clients, and grow accounts — without the chaos.

FAQs

We've heard every concern. Here's what you really need to know.

Huy leads every engagement from strategy, creative direction, and your primary point of contact throughout. Depending on scope, we bring in specialized collaborators for development, 3D, and motion design.

Most projects run between 8-12 weeks from kickoff to launch. This includes strategy, design, development, and QA. We never rush the process because quality compounds when every layer earns its place.

Our projects typically start from $15,000 AUD for a focused brand and web engagement. Every project is scoped individually based on your specific needs and goals. We'll provide a clear proposal before any commitment.

We work primarily with established founder-led brands who have proven their concept and are ready to elevate their presence. If you're pre-revenue, we're probably not the right fit yet.

We're not a factory. We take on fewer projects so we can give each one everything. Huy leads every engagement personally, and our obsession with closing the gap between who you are and how the world sees you drives every decision.
