The NoGood Book
Publication

My illustrations, through the rickety hardware and clunky modded devices they feature, are informed by notions of ownership and control.

I established NoGood in 2021. More than a pseudonym, I found that NoGood provided a space to experiment. Since then, my commissioned projects and my personal work started to blend, establishing a throughline that spans multiple years.
